About Crystal View Residency Hotel

Crystal View Residency was incorporated on 15-Nov-2022

Experience the finest hotel stay in Guruvayoor at Crystal Residency, where comfort meets elegance. Our hotel offers affordable accommodation with well-appointed rooms designed to provide a serene and cozy atmosphere. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and luxurious bedding to ensure a restful night's sleep.

Located just a short distance from the renowned Guruvayoor Temple, Crystal Residency is the perfect choice for pilgrims, families, and business travelers alike. Whether you're here for a spiritual journey, a relaxing family vacation, or a productive business trip, we are committed to making your stay memorable.
